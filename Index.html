ECHOES OF TOMORROW — THE FILM

NOVA

Electronic Synthwave Alt-Pop Cinematic

Born in the neon-lit underground of Berlin's electronic scene, NOVA emerged as a sonic architect reshaping the boundaries between synthetic textures and raw human emotion.

With three platinum albums and sold-out arena tours spanning five continents, NOVA has become the defining voice of a generation that lives between digital dreams and analog reality.

"Echoes of Tomorrow" represents the culmination of two years of sonic exploration — recorded across studios in Tokyo, Reykjavik, and Los Angeles, each track is a window into possible futures where technology and humanity find harmony.
